About

I like the work between the raw data and the dashboard.

My focus is on what happens after the JSON arrives and before someone opens a chart. The part that gets called "plumbing" until it breaks. ETL pipelines, dimensional models, feature engineering, the retry loop that saves the nightly job from a flaky upstream API.

I started in IT at MicroCenter deploying networks for small-business clients, which taught me to treat operational reliability as a feature, not an afterthought. I just finished my Data Science degree at Berkeley after transferring from Golden West College.
